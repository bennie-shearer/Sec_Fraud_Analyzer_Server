Third-Party Acknowledgments:

This project implements algorithms from the following academic works:

1. Beneish M-Score
   Beneish, M.D. (1999). "The Detection of Earnings Manipulation."
   Financial Analysts Journal, 55(5), 24-36.

2. Altman Z-Score
   Altman, E.I. (1968). "Financial Ratios, Discriminant Analysis and the
   Prediction of Corporate Bankruptcy." Journal of Finance, 23(4), 589-609.

3. Piotroski F-Score
   Piotroski, J.D. (2000). "Value Investing: The Use of Historical Financial
   Statement Information to Separate Winners from Losers."
   Journal of Accounting Research, 38, 1-41.

4. Benford's Law
   Nigrini, M.J. (2012). Benford's Law: Applications for Forensic Accounting,
   Auditing, and Fraud Detection. Wiley.

5. Fraud Triangle
   Cressey, D.R. (1953). Other People's Money: A Study in the Social
   Psychology of Embezzlement. Free Press.
